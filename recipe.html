Chocolate Cake Recipe

Guided Chocolate Cake Recipe

This delicious gooey chocolate cake recipe will help you create your own

Ingredient

  • 8oz Butter
  • 8oz sugar
  • 4 eggs
  • 8oz flour
  • 2oz cocoa powder
  • 4tbsp vegetable oil
  • 3 tbsp golden syrup
  • 4tbsp milk
  • 2 dairy milk chocolate

Method

  1. Whisk sugar and butter until fluffy and white
  2. whisk eggs separetly and then add to butter mixture
  3. Add vegetable oil, golden syrup and milk to the mixture
  4. Add flour and cocoa powder and fold in carefully
  5. Pour into tin.
  6. preheat oven to 170 degrees and bake for 25 minutes
  7. check oven after 20minutes
  8. use a knife to pierce into cake.
    If it bounces back, then it is cooked
  9. Add the dairy milk chocolate onto the cake while it is still hot
  10. spread the chocolate across the cake
  11. plate up and serve
