Chocolate Cake Recipe

Prep Time: 20mins
Cook Time: 1 hour

Ingredients

  • 8oz Butter
  • 8oz sugar
  • 4 eggs
  • 8oz flour
  • 2oz cocoa powder
  • 4tbsp vegetable oil
  • 3 tbsp golden syrup
  • 4tbsp milk
  • 2 dairy milk chocolate

Method

  • Whisk sugar and butter until fluffy and white
  • whisk eggs separetly and then add to butter mixture
  • Add vegetable oil, golden syrup and milk to the mixture
  • Add flour and cocoa powder and fold in carefully
  • Pour into tin.
  • preheat oven to 170 degrees and bake for 25 minutes
  • check oven after 20minutes
  • use a knife to pierce into cake.If it bounces back, then it is cooked
  • Add the dairy milk chocolate onto the cake while it is still hot
  • spread the chocolate across the cake
  • plate up and serve
